Privacy policy of Human Resource Development (HRD)

Human Resource Development (HRD) collects information about its visitors to help get proper job as their experience and background and connect with employers. We use this information for business purposes and to let employers know the details of job seekers as employers get their right employee and the employees can get hired for their right job.

How we collect information:

We collect information about you from our website where you submit your data and resume purposing signing in or applying any job you want to get hired. We can also get your information when you submit your any data on our HRD’s facebook, linkedIn or Google+ page.

How we use information:

We use your information for the services you apply. As a Human Resource consultancy firm, we ask demand of product and services from third parties as we can provide them capable employees. In accordance with experience and background we forward the information of job seekers to the third party. We forward data only to those third parties who have singed a “Service Legal Agreement” or “Placement Strategy Agreement” with us. We give highest priority of your information you share, we save and provide your information to the third parties maintaining a safest channel. We provide your information only to those third parties where you apply for job or you are interested to do as they can contact with you. After sharing your information with third parties, with your consent, HRD is not liable to the safety of your information.

Third parties or HRD can call the reference people you give your resume or reference field of our website to know about you and your academic and service background.

We can disclose your information where legally required or subject to disclose under law.

Confidentiality and Security of information:

You will have full control over your information after sharing it with us even you can customize it whether it is searchable or not. Your CV will be available for the third parties only when you apply for their products, services or jobs. You can also block any employer as you want and remove your CV from server whenever you want and even you can delete your HRD ID at any time. If any third parties breach our terms about any information, we immediately terminate the contract with them.

We use strong tools and techniques like encryption, password and physical security to protect your personal information against unauthorized access and disclosure. We regularly revise and update the privacy policy and publish at our website.
